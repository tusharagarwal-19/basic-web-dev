School of Computer Engineering

The School of Computer Engineering (SCE) at KIIT University is renowned for its excellence in research and teaching. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, focusing on cutting-edge technologies and industry demands.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on practical knowledge, SCE prepares students for careers in software development, artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and more.

Key highlights of the School:

  • World-class laboratories and computing facilities
  • Collaborations with leading tech companies
  • Innovative curriculum aligned with global standards
  • Active research in AI, Machine Learning, IoT, and Cloud Computing
